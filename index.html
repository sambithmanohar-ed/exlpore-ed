Brain Basics

Start with the big picture: major lobes and what they do.

Cerebral Lobes

  • Frontal: planning, decisions, speech (Broca’s area).
  • Parietal: touch, spatial awareness.
  • Temporal: hearing, language (Wernicke’s), memory.
  • Occipital: vision processing.

Tip: A blow to the back of the head can cause visual flashes—occipital cortex at work.

Key Structures

  • Hippocampus: new memories (think “campus” for learning).
  • Amygdala: emotion & fear detection.
  • Cerebellum: balance, fine motor skills, timing.
  • Brainstem: breathing, heart rate, basic life support.
